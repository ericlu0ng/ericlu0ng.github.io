Education ~ Courses

Introduction to Financial Accouting: An introductory course to Financial Accouting. In this class, I studied various financial statements such as: Balance Sheets, Income Statements and Statement of Cash Flow. Futher analysis of these statements included analysis of: Bad Debt, Bonds, and Depreciation. Thoughout the course, I completed weekly analysis of the items above, enhancing my understanding and knowledge.

Introduction to Functional Programing: A course on Functional Programing through Scheme and DrRacket. Over the term, I analyzed and practised using the functional programs Sceme and DrRacket. The course mainly focused on the abstraction of Lists, Trees, Graphs, and Structures. A prerequisite to my current class, Introduction to Imperative Porgraming.

Linear Algebra for Honors Mathematics: A great course on linear algebra as well as fundamentals to mathematics. Mathematical logic behind proofs were studied with great detail. Throughout the course, various proof techniques such as induction and contradiction were practiced.
